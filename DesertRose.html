香氣輪廓 Fragrance Profile

Desert Rose 介於花香的柔軟與沙漠熱風之間。開場是玫瑰與香料，之後逐漸轉為乾燥木質，最後停留在沉香深沉的氣息裡。 Desert Rose sits between floral softness and desert heat. It opens with rose and spice, settles into dry woods, and lingers with a deep oud signature.

氛圍印象 Atmosphere

靈感來自乾燥地景中的一朵玫瑰。它不著重甜美，而是描繪柔軟與熱度、花瓣與風沙、安靜與深度之間的對比。 Inspired by rose blooming against an arid horizon, Desert Rose is less about sweetness and more about contrast: softness beside heat, petals against wind, stillness against depth.

  • 暖辛香 Warm spice
  • 乾燥雪松 Dry cedar
  • 煙燻沉香 Smoky oud
  • 柔和麝香 Soft musk
  • 深色玫瑰 Dark rose

香調結構 Fragrance Notes

這支香氣以三個層次展開，在皮膚上緩慢推進，讓每一段都有不同的質地與溫度。 Built in three quiet movements, each layer reveals a different side of the scent as it settles on skin.

前調 Top Notes

玫瑰 · 孜然 Rose · Cumin

開場先是玫瑰的柔軟，接著由孜然帶出乾燥而辛熱的邊緣感，形成這支香水最鮮明的第一印象。 The opening is vivid and warm. Rose appears first, then cumin adds a dry, spiced edge that gives the fragrance its desert heat.

中調 Heart Notes

玫瑰 · 香根草 · 雪松 Rose · Vetiver · Cedarwood

來到中段後，玫瑰變得更深、更有質地，香根草與雪松讓整體輪廓更乾淨俐落，帶出木質的骨架。 At the center, rose deepens into something more textured. Vetiver and cedarwood create a dry, structured heart with subtle green and woody depth.

後調 Base Notes

麝香 · 愈創木 · 沉香 Musk · Guaiac Wood · Oud

收尾平滑而安靜，麝香柔化整體輪廓，愈創木與沉香則留下帶煙感、深色且持久的尾韻。 The drydown is smooth, smoky, and intimate. Musk softens the composition while guaiac wood and oud leave a dark, lingering trail.

香氣故事 Scent Story

這是一片被氣味記住的地景，不依靠影像，而是靠空氣、質地與溫度被想起。 A landscape remembered not through images, but through air, texture, and warmth.

地景 The Landscape

當夕陽落在無盡沙丘上，空氣開始安靜下來，只剩下餘溫、風與木質氣息在地表緩慢流動。就在這片乾燥之中，一朵玫瑰安靜綻放。 As the sun lowers over endless dunes, the air becomes still and full of warmth. Dry wind carries traces of spice and wood across the sand, while somewhere unexpected, a rose blooms.

印象 The Impression

Desert Rose 捕捉的是一種對比：花瓣的柔軟與地景的嚴酷、沉香的深度與玫瑰的表面光澤、克制與餘韻之間的平衡。 Desert Rose captures that contrast: the softness of petals against a harsh horizon, the quiet richness of oud beneath a floral surface, the elegance of restraint instead of excess.

情緒 The Emotion

這不是一支明亮甜美的玫瑰，而是一支更深色、更安靜的玫瑰。它貼近肌膚時顯得內斂，離開之後卻會留下記憶點。 This is not a bright rose. It is a deeper one — shaded, warm, and composed. A scent that feels intimate at first, then leaves a memorable trace behind.

定位 The Signature

在 NAHA 的 Landscape Collection 中，Desert Rose 是一幅關於沙漠熱風與花香陰影的肖像，神秘、克制，卻有明確存在感。 In the NAHA Landscape Collection, Desert Rose stands as a portrait of desert air and floral shadow: mysterious, polished, and quietly intense.

「一朵被熱度托起、被風雕塑，最終留存在木質記憶裡的玫瑰。」 “A rose carried by heat, shaped by wind, and remembered in wood.”
